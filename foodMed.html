What is Mediterranean Cuisine?

Mediterranean cuisine is a delightful and healthy culinary tradition from the Mediterranean region. It emphasizes fresh ingredients like olive oil, vegetables, seafood, and aromatic herbs, resulting in flavorful dishes that are good for both the body and the soul.

Mouthwatering Dishes

Tabouli

Tabouli is a refreshing Mediterranean salad made with finely chopped parsley, tomatoes, mint, onion, and soaked bulgur. It's dressed with olive oil and lemon juice, creating a zesty and nutritious dish.

Hummus

Hummus is a creamy dip or spread prepared from chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. It's a beloved Mediterranean classic, perfect for dipping fresh pita bread or as a flavorful side dish.
